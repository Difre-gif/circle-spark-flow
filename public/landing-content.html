The Problem

Rent collection is a monthly
administrative nightmare.

Rwandan landlords are savvy businesspeople. The problem is not skill — it's tooling. No platform was ever built for how East Africa actually pays rent.

💬

WhatsApp screenshot chaos

20 tenants. 20 phone numbers. 20 different screenshots of MoMo confirmations — all arriving out of order throughout the month.

⚠️

Fake payment fraud

Tenants send edited screenshots claiming they paid. Without a verification system, landlords have been defrauded repeatedly.

📁

No audit trail, no reports

Month-end means manually reconciling Excel sheets, WhatsApp chats, and a phone inbox. It takes days, not minutes.

🌐

Every existing tool ignores MoMo

South African, Western, and even Kenyan tools treat MTN MoMo as an afterthought — or don't support it at all.

"I spend three days every month chasing payments. I need one place where I can see everything — who paid, who didn't, and the transaction ID to prove it."

How It Works

From MoMo payment to verified receipt
in three steps.

No new apps for your tenants. No complex setup for you. Just a clean, reliable system that replaces the chaos.

01
📱

Tenant pays via MoMo

Your tenant pays rent the way they always have — MTN MoMo USSD. Then they log into BizRent's simple tenant portal and submit their MoMo transaction ID and optional screenshot.

02
✅

You verify in one click

The payment appears in your dashboard's pending queue with the full transaction ID visible. You verify it's real, then approve or reject in one click. Every action is timestamped and logged.

03
📄

Receipt delivered automatically

The moment you approve, BizRent generates a professional PDF receipt and delivers it to your tenant. At month-end, download a full PDF or CSV report for your accountant in one click.
